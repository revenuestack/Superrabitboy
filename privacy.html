Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Super Rabbit Boy is a single-player offline game. We take your privacy seriously and have designed the app to work entirely on your device without collecting any data.

Information We Collect

None. Super Rabbit Boy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any external servers.

Local Data

All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Super Rabbit Boy does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Super Rabbit Boy does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. The app contains no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no in-app purchases. It is designed to be safe and appropriate for players of all ages.

Advertising

Super Rabbit Boy contains no advertising of any kind.

In-App Purchases

Super Rabbit Boy contains no in-app purchases.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at superrabbitboygame@gmail.com.
