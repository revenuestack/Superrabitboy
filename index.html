About the Game

Run, jump, and stomp your way through 6 action-packed worlds! Super Rabbit Boy must race across Animal Town, dive through Splash Slash Sea, brave the Sandstorm Desert, bounce through Cloudy Hills, survive Mount Boom, and battle through Boom Boom Factory to stop King Viking!

Features

6 unique themed worlds with handcrafted pixel-art levels. Tight, responsive controls built for touchscreen play. Unlock power-ups as you progress. Epic boss battle against King Viking. No ads. No in-app purchases. Just pure platforming fun.
