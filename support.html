FAQ

What devices does the game support?
Super Rabbit Boy is designed for iPad running iOS 17 or later.

How do I control the character?
Use the on-screen buttons: left and right arrows to move, and the jump button to jump. You can stomp enemies by landing on top of them.

How do I save my progress?
Touch a checkpoint flag during a level to save your position. If you fall or get hit, you'll respawn at the last checkpoint.

What are the carrots for?
Collecting a carrot gives you a temporary speed and jump boost. Use it to reach tricky platforms!

How do power-ups work?
You unlock power-ups by completing levels. After each unlock, you can choose which power-up to use for the next level.

The game feels laggy or slow.
Try closing other apps running in the background. Super Rabbit Boy works best when your iPad has plenty of free memory.
